Volunteering

We work with thousands of volunteers around the world. In GreenLa different departments for volunteers exist:

  • Financial Department
  • Human Resources Department
  • PR Department
  • Design Department

And the best part is that HR Dep. is here to help you fit in the right category that suits your interests and skills the best. If you complete and submit the form attached to the right side with your name and email adress we will contact you in order to give you more details about the program. Afterwards we can schedule an online meeting and get you know you!
